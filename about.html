About Pawfect Pets

Pawfect Pets is a fictional pet supplies store built as a demo project. Our mission is to make it easy for pet owners to find high-quality items for their dogs and cats. This project demonstrates a small e-commerce front-end with product listings, product detail pages, and a simple cart using localStorage.
